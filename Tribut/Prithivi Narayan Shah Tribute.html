Tribute to Prithvi Narayan Shah

Early Life

Prithvi Narayan Shah was born in Gorkha, Nepal in 1723. From a young age, he showed signs of great leadership and a strong sense of duty towards his people.

First King of Nepal

Determined to unify the fragmented kingdoms of Nepal, Prithvi Narayan Shah embarked on a mission to bring together various principalities under a single rule. His military campaigns and diplomatic strategies were pivotal in achieving this goal.
